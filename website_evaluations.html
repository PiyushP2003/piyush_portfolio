Website Evaluations

Best Buy

Best Buy is a leading retailer offering a wide range of electronics and appliances.

  • Contrast: Good
  • Repetition: Consistent use of elements
  • Alignment: Elements are aligned well
  • Proximity: Grouping of related items

The Best Buy website demonstrates good use of CRAP principles with clear contrast, repetition, alignment, and proximity.

Craigslist Charlotte

Craigslist is a classified advertisements website covering various categories.

  • Contrast: Limited color contrast
  • Repetition: Minimal consistent styling
  • Alignment: Basic alignment of elements
  • Proximity: Items are listed but limited grouping

The Craigslist Charlotte website has some limitations in terms of CRAP principles, with minimal styling and limited color contrast.
